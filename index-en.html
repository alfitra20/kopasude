Never heard of Kopasude?, here is a little about us.

Kopasude Foundation is a community forum in the city of Medan which focused on building awareness for children, society and the environment around the Deli river.

  • Education
  • Community Business Development
  • Environmental Conservation
  • Community Assistance

Why Deli River?

Deli River is one of the main rivers in the River Basin Unit (SWS) Belawan / Yetai Snake with 5 (five) tributaries. Deli river along with its children and its branches flow from Karo Regency, Deli Serdang Regency and crossing Medan City before disembogue into the Malacca Strait.

Environmental Conservation around the Deli river is very important. however, socialization, education and the welfare of riverbank communities are no less important, because they can have a direct impact on the sustainability and progress of the city of Medan.

There are 4 Kopasude's development areas which have been implemented.


Urban Forest Zone

Located at the floodgate of the canal in Johor, this area will become an urban forest area, BWS S II this area has been planted with 1250 tree seedlings since last November 2017.


Sanggar Pendidikan
Silaturahmi

In the village of Badur, a studio has been formed which is managed independently by residents, and until now Kopasude still routinely conducts teaching for 6 times in a week, including hands-on training and Waste Bank Development.


Homestay Kopasude

In this area, we designed a village pilot that we called Kopasude Homestay or Green Village Kopasude. There, the arrangement of slums was carried out to create a green area that is friendly for children.


Aquaculture Pond

Kopasude Conducts Training on Cultivation of consumption fish to build the creative economy of Deli River Basin residents in Marelan and conducting environmentally friendly socialization.

Help and Support

Kopasude received support from the North Sumatra Provincial Government, Medan City Government, Volunteers and many other parties. also, the communities around the Deli River whom very cooperative and helpful the realization of our programs.
